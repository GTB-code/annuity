Bonds belong in the 401(k)

In the brokerage, bond interest is taxed as ordinary income every single year. Inside the 401(k) it compounds untouched until you draw it.

Holding the same 60 · 25 · 15 with bonds relocated saves roughly $400 a year in tax, for zero change in risk.
